# General SDD Review Checklist

## Requirements quality
- [ ] Problem and goal are explicit and non-contradictory.
- [ ] Scope and out-of-scope are explicit.
- [ ] Edge cases are listed and testable.
- [ ] Acceptance criteria use observable language (prefer EARS).

## Design quality
- [ ] Architecture is consistent with constraints.
- [ ] Tradeoffs and rejected options are documented.
- [ ] Failure handling and rollback are defined.

## Task quality
- [ ] Tasks are atomic and executable in sequence.
- [ ] Each task includes verification (test or manual check).
- [ ] Dependencies and ordering are clear.

## Safety and delivery
- [ ] Open questions and assumptions are tracked.
- [ ] Security/compliance constraints are addressed.
- [ ] Docs are concise and free of stale sections.
